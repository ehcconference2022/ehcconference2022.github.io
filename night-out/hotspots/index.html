    Having trouble deciding where to go? Head to these districts full of options and you are bound to find a place that appeals to your senses.

    Meatpacking District

    Once home to Copenhagen´s meat industry businesses, this industrial areas has now transformed into a buzzing creative cluster of galleries, nifghtlife and restaurants. Craft beers, wine or cocktails? - you will find it all here. And if a hunger develops, there are plenty of options to remedy that.

    Værnedamsvej

    Straddling the border between hip Vesterbro and posh Frederiksberg,the street Værnedamsvej has a distinct Parisian vibe to it. If you are looking for a cozy, relaxed spot to have a drink, this is the top spot to find it.
