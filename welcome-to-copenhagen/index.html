    Finally we meet again!

    Welcome to Copenhagen

    It seems like an eternity since we last met in real life at the EHC conference - all the way back in Skopje in 2019. Therefore we are so excited to welcome you all to Copenhagen, the capital city of Denmark. The 2022 EHC Conference will be a chance to catch up on the lost time, revitalize old friendships and make new ones, and to absorb and discuss the issues pertinent to our community.

    Even though there is a tightly packed and highly interesting conference programme, we would encourage you to use the breaks to explore the wonderful city. If you are looking for inspiration, this guide is here to help you.
